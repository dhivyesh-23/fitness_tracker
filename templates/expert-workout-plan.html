Expert Level Weekly Workout Plan

Goal: Maximize muscle hypertrophy, strength, and endurance through the most demanding training methods.

Day 1: Upper Body Power

  • Flat Barbell Bench Press – 5 sets x 4-6 reps
  • Weighted Pull-Ups – 4 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Pendlay Rows – 4 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Dips (Weighted) – 3 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Barbell Curls – 4 sets x 8-10 reps (superset with Triceps)
  • Skull Crushers – 4 sets x 10-12 reps

Rest 90-120s between heavy compound sets. Emphasize explosive concentric movement.

Day 2: Lower Body Max Effort

  • Barbell Back Squats – 5 sets x 3-5 reps
  • Deadlifts – 4 sets x 4-6 reps
  • Front Squats – 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 4 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Walking Lunges with Dumbbells – 3 sets x 20 steps
  • Standing Calf Raises – 4 sets x 15-20 reps

Use lifting straps and belt if needed. Focus on tempo (3-1-1).

Day 3: Functional Conditioning

  • Sled Push – 6 rounds x 30m
  • Tire Flips – 5 rounds x 10 reps
  • Farmer’s Walk (Heavy) – 4 rounds x 40m
  • Rope Climbs – 5 reps x max height
  • Kettlebell Swings – 5 sets x 20 reps
  • Battle Ropes – 30s on / 30s off x 8 rounds

Full-body focus. Keep rest minimal to elevate heart rate and mental endurance.

Day 4: Hypertrophy Chest & Arms

  • Incline Smith Machine Press – 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Cable Flyes (Drop Set) – 3 sets x 15 + drop x 10
  • Close-Grip Bench Press – 4 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Preacher Curls – 4 sets x 12-15 reps
  • EZ Bar Curl – 3 sets x 10-12 reps (superset)
  • Overhead Dumbbell Extension – 3 sets x 12-15 reps
  • Incline Hammer Curls – 2 sets x 20 reps

Perform supersets with minimal rest. Focus on squeezing the muscle at peak contraction.

Day 5: Hypertrophy Back & Shoulders

  • Weighted Pull-Ups – 5 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows – 4 sets x 12 reps
  • Lat Pulldown (Behind Neck) – 3 sets x 15 reps
  • Arnold Press – 4 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Lateral Raises (Drop Set) – 4 x 12 + 8 + 6
  • Face Pulls – 4 sets x 15-20 reps
  • Barbell Shrugs – 4 sets x 15 reps

Focus on wide grip and deep stretch for lat work. Strict form on shoulder isolation movements.

Day 6: Full Body High Volume

  • Clean & Press – 4 sets x 6 reps
  • Trap Bar Deadlifts – 4 sets x 10 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats – 3 sets x 12 reps per leg
  • Chin-Ups (Slow Tempo) – 4 sets x 8 reps
  • Chest Dips – 3 sets x 15-20 reps
  • Barbell Complex (Deadlift, Row, Hang Clean, Front Squat, Push Press) – 3 rounds x 6 reps each

No rest between barbell complex exercises. Use moderate weight.

Day 7: Recovery & Mobility

  • Active Stretching – 20 minutes
  • Foam Rolling – 15 minutes
  • Yoga Flow – 30 minutes
  • Sauna or Cold Plunge – Optional
  • Deep Tissue Massage – Optional

This day is essential for maintaining recovery. Focus on breathing and mindfulness.
