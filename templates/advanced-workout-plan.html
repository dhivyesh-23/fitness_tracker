Advanced Weekly Workout Plan

Monday – Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • Flat Barbell Bench Press – 4x6-8
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 4x8-10
  • Seated Overhead Press – 4x6-8
  • Lateral Raises – 4x12-15
  • Overhead Cable Triceps Extensions – 4x12-15

Tuesday – Pull (Back, Biceps)

  • Pull-Ups – 4xAMRAP
  • Barbell Rows – 4x6-8
  • Chest-Supported T-Bar Rows – 4x10-12
  • Incline Dumbbell Curls – 3x10-12
  • Hammer Curls – 3x12-15

Wednesday – Legs (Glutes, Hamstrings, Quads)

  • Barbell Back Squats – 4x6-8
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 4x8-10
  • Walking Lunges – 3x10-12 (each leg)
  • Leg Extensions – 3x15-20
  • Calf Raises – 4x15-20

Thursday – Rest or Active Recovery

  • Light cardio, yoga, or mobility work

Rest and prepare for heavy lifts tomorrow.

Friday – Powerlifting Focus (Full Body)

  • Deadlifts – 5x3 (focus on low rep, high intensity)
  • Barbell Overhead Press – 4x6-8
  • Front Squats – 4x6-8
  • Weighted Pull-Ups – 3xAMRAP
  • Dips – 3x10-12

Saturday – Hypertrophy Focus (Upper)

  • Incline Barbell Bench Press – 4x8-10
  • Lat Pulldowns – 4x10-12
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3x10-12
  • Dumbbell Chest Flyes – 3x12-15
  • EZ-Bar Curls – 3x12-15
  • Triceps Rope Pushdowns – 3x15-20

Sunday – Rest or Mobility

  • Foam rolling, stretching, and/or light swimming or walking

Optional: light cardio or mindfulness practice
